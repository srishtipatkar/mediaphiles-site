1. HERO SECTION IMPROVEMENTS

Current Issue:
The hero section text does not stand out enough against the background image. The background image is visually too dominant, making the headline and subheadline harder to read.

Required Fixes:

- Reduce the visual intensity of the hero background image by 30–40%.
- Add a dark overlay (black or dark charcoal) with approximately 55–70% opacity.
- Increase contrast between text and background.
- Make the headline significantly more prominent.
- Increase font weight of the headline.
- Add subtle text shadow or backdrop blur behind text if necessary.
- Ensure readability on both desktop and mobile.
- The hero section should immediately draw attention to the message, not the image.

Desired Feel:
Luxury hospitality brand website similar to Aman Resorts, Six Senses, or Four Seasons.

The hierarchy should be:

1. Headline
2. Subheadline
3. CTA Buttons
4. Background Image

Not the other way around.
